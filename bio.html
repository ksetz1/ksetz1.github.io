Kolbe Setzer is a recent graduate with a B.S. in Information Systems from the University of Maryland Baltimore County Honors University (UMBC). Kolbe has skills in operating systems such as Microsoft Windows and MAC OS, database management including SQL, PL/SQL, and Oracle DBMS, programming languages such as Java, HTML, JavaScript, and Python, and various software including Microsoft Office Suite, JGrasp, Visual Studio Code, Adobe Premiere, Final Cut Pro, and Quickbooks.

Kolbe has also been actively involved in extracurricular activities such as serving as the Treasurer and Videographer of the Black Men's Society and the Lead Ambassador, Event Coordinator, and Videographer of the Black Student Union.
